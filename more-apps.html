SignalHire

Cuts through the noise

An independent job discovery platform that solves choice overload in job searching. Unlike traditional job boards and social networks, this system aggregates opportunities from company career pages and applies advanced filtering based on candidate priorities: salary transparency, visa sponsorship, remote policies, tech stack alignment, and seniority level. Delivers curated, explainable job alerts via email/app.
